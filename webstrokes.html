    Welcome to our Website

    Strokes is the official art and photography club of NIT Durgapur. It aims at patronizing the sense of creativity among the students of NIT Durgapur. We organize KALAKRITI (The Annual art and photography exhibition), PHOTO WALKS, WORKSHOPS and many more events throughout the year.
